# What Should I Wear Today? — Practical Guide

Choosing what to wear is a daily decision that depends on multiple factors including weather, occasion, and personal style.

This guide explains how to make better outfit decisions quickly and consistently.

---

## Key Factors to Consider

### Weather
- Temperature
- Rain or wind
- Seasonal conditions

### Occasion
- Work
- Casual
- Formal events
- Social settings

### Personal Comfort
- Fit and comfort
- Mood
- Confidence

---

## Simple Decision Framework

1. Check the weather  
2. Identify the occasion  
3. Select a base outfit  
4. Add layers if needed  
5. Choose appropriate shoes and accessories  

---

## Common Problems

- Decision fatigue
- Overlooking available clothing
- Repeating the same outfits
- Not dressing appropriately for weather

---

## How to Make It Easier

- Plan outfits in advance
- Organize your wardrobe
- Focus on versatile pieces
- Track what you wear
