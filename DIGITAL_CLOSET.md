# Digital Closet — Complete Guide

A digital closet is a system that allows users to store, organize, and manage their clothing in a digital format.

It replaces a traditional wardrobe overview with a structured, searchable, and interactive system.

---

## What is a Digital Closet?

A digital closet is a virtual representation of your physical wardrobe.

Each clothing item is stored as a structured object with attributes such as:

- Category (e.g. tops, bottoms, shoes)
- Color
- Style
- Brand
- Purchase price
- Wear history

---

## How Digital Closets Work

Digital closets typically follow this process:

1. Users upload clothing items (photos or imports)
2. Items are categorized and tagged
3. Data is stored in a structured system
4. Users can browse, filter, and search their wardrobe

---

## Key Features

Modern digital closets often include:

- Clothing categorization
- Search and filtering
- Outfit creation tools
- Wear tracking
- Visual wardrobe overview

---

## Benefits

- Better visibility of owned clothing
- Easier outfit planning
- Reduced duplicate purchases
- Improved organization
- Increased usage of existing items

---

## Common Limitations

Basic digital closets may:

- Require manual input for all items
- Lack intelligent recommendations
- Not track usage over time
- Provide limited insights

---

## Digital Closet vs Traditional Wardrobe

| Feature | Digital Closet | Physical Wardrobe |
|--------|---------------|------------------|
| Searchable | Yes | No |
| Organized by data | Yes | Limited |
| Usage tracking | Yes | No |
| Outfit planning | Yes | Manual |
